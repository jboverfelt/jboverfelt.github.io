About

I’m a software developer who enjoys working at all points in the stack, from working with hardware to configuring servers and databases to writing frontend code. I currently work in Go for backend code and Vue.js for frontend development, though I have extensive Java experience along with some Ruby and Clojure.

I am an avid ham radio operator (callsign AB3E), active primarily on HF, with some local work on VHF and above.

Feel free to drop me a line at my public inbox (link below) or my email on QRZ if you’re a ham.

I also maintain a phlog (A blog using the Gopher protocol) here:

gopher://colorfield.space/1/~jboverf
